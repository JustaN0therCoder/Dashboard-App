The Beach Boys Tickets Make Great Valentine's Day Gifts

The Beach Boys are coming to the Extraco Events Center on March 27, 2019 at 7:30 p.m. Tickets are on sale now in time for Valentine’s Day presents and can be purchased at the Extraco Events Center Box Office and at ExtracoEventsCenter.com.

 

Captained by Mike Love, The Beach Boys have continued to create and perform with the same bold imagination and style that marked their explosive debut over 50 years ago. Few acts, if any, can match The Beach Boys’ concert presence, spirit and performance.

 

Complete with “Surfin’,” The Beach Boys’ first hit, along with “Fun, Fun, Fun,” “I Get Around,” “Help Me Rhonda,” “California Girls,” “Kokomo” and the Grammy nominated “Good Vibrations,” this concert is sure to please generations of music fans.

 

Make sure you pick up some "Good Vibrations" at The Beach Boys: Now & Then Tour on March 27 at the Extraco Events Center!

 

Visit ExtracoEventsCenter.com to purchase your tickets today!

Waco 100 Texas Music Blowout

Waco 100 and Bud Light present the Waco 100 Blowout, featuring Brit Kerr, Parker McCollum and Turnpike Troubadours on February 16, 2019 at the Extraco Events Center!

 

Catch three of the hottest bands in Texas Country take the stage in the Extraco Coliseum! Tickets on sale NOW! A VIP ticket is $75 and includes a seat at a table; please note seating is not reserved, first-come, first-served. A general admission floor ticket is $30 (standing room only) and a general admission balcony ticket is $25.

 

Tickets can be purchased at the Extraco Events Center Box Office or online at ExtracoEventsCenter.com.

O'Reilly Auto Parts Outlaw Nationals

See six of the most awesome metal munching monster truck superstars in all-out war at the Extraco Events Center on February 22 and 23. Witness the biggest rivalry ever REIGNITED! - BIGFOOT versus BEARFOOT, plus Monster Patrol, Tuff-E--Nuff, Tantrum and Texas's own King Krunch. Be there for Racing, Wheelie Competition and Freestyle like never before! Plus, don’t forget to take a ride on the Sergeant Smash Ride Truck.

 

See local weekend warriors and professionals from around the country battle it out on the treacherous course. Think you have a Tuff Truck? Call 417-887-9400 for more information and to get your truck in the show. Pro and stock classes will be included for 2019!

 

Doors open at 6 p.m. each night and the shows start at 7:30 p.m. Tickets are on sale now at the Extraco Events Center Box Office or online at ExtracoEventsCenter.com.
